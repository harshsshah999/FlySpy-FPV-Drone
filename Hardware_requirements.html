Hardware Component Functional Requirements

Hardware Component Function / Purpose Requirement Justification
Flight Controller (SpeedyBee F405 AIO) Central processing unit for flight control and ESC management. Supports Betaflight; integrated ESC saves space and weight; suitable for FPV and experimentation.
Electronic Speed Controller (ESC) Controls the power delivered to motors. Integrated in FC; must handle up to 35A for high-performance motors.
Motors (Xing E Pro 2207 2750KV ×4) Provide thrust and maneuverability for flight. High KV rating for responsive FPV control; compatible with 5” propellers.
Frame (FlyFishRC Volador II VX5 T700) Houses and protects all components; affects weight and stability. Lightweight carbon fiber; optimized layout for freestyle and FPV setups.
Propellers (51477 & Ethix S4 PC) Convert motor spin into lift and thrust. Chosen for 5-inch frames; mix of efficiency and control; supports smooth flight.
FPV Camera (Caddx Ratel Pro) Captures real-time video for pilot or AI vision input. High-quality image with low-light capability; ideal for both FPV and object detection.
Video Transmitter (SpeedyBee TX800) Sends FPV camera signal to goggles. Reliable video range; adjustable power output; Betaflight-compatible.
Receiver (Radiomaster RP1 ELRS) Receives commands from the remote controller. Low latency and long range; compatible with ELRS protocol.
Remote Controller (Radiomaster Pocket) Manual pilot control input. Compact, full-featured, open-source firmware; supports ELRS.
FPV Goggles (Skyzone Cobra X V4) Display live FPV video to the pilot. High-resolution screen with DVR; supports analog video.
Main Battery (Tattu R-Line 650mAh 4S 95C) Provides power to motors and FC. High discharge rate needed for powerful motors and responsive control.
Accessory Batteries (NCR 400mAh) Powers SBC and peripheral components. Separate power source for stability and system reliability.
Smoke Stopper (TBS Avionics) Protects components from short circuits during testing. Reduces risk of damage during initial power-ups and troubleshooting.
